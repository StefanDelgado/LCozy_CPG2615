## üéØ System Overview

### What is LCozy?

LCozy is a comprehensive dormitory management system designed to streamline the entire process of managing dormitories, from booking to payment processing. The system serves three primary user types:

- **Students**: Browse and book dormitories, manage payments, communicate with owners
- **Owners**: Manage properties, handle bookings, track payments and tenants
- **Administrators**: Oversee the entire system, verify users, manage reports

### Key Features

‚úÖ **Multi-Platform Access**: Web and mobile application support  
‚úÖ **Real-Time Booking**: Instant booking requests and approvals  
‚úÖ **Payment Tracking**: Complete payment lifecycle management  
‚úÖ **Messaging System**: Direct communication between students and owners  
‚úÖ **Review System**: Rate and review dormitory experiences  
‚úÖ **Location-Based Search**: Find dorms near you with map integration  
‚úÖ **Multi-User Management**: Role-based access control

### System Requirements

**Web Platform:**
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- Recommended screen resolution: 1366x768 or higher

**Mobile Application:**
- Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher
- iOS 11.0 or higher
- Internet connection
- Minimum 100MB free storage

### Payment Management

#### Understanding Payment Types

**1. Deposit Payment**
- One-time payment upon booking approval
- Typically equivalent to 1 month's rent
- Refundable at checkout (subject to conditions)

**2. Monthly Rent**
- Regular monthly payment
- Due on the same date each month
- Sent as payment reminders

#### Viewing Payment History

1. Go to **"Payments"** from your dashboard
2. View all payments with:
   - Payment ID and type
   - Amount and due date
   - Status (Pending, Submitted, Paid, Overdue)
   - Associated dorm and room
   - Days until due / Days overdue

**Payment Statistics:**
- Total pending payments
- Overdue count
- Total amount due
- Total paid amount

#### Making a Payment

**Step 1: Find Pending Payment**
- Locate the payment with "Pending" status
- Note the due date and amount

**Step 2: Make Payment via Bank/GCash/PayMaya**
- Transfer the exact amount to the owner's account
- Keep the transaction receipt/screenshot

**Step 3: Upload Receipt**
1. Click **"Upload Receipt"** on the payment card
2. Choose image from your device
3. Ensure receipt shows:
   - Transaction date and time
   - Amount transferred
   - Reference number
4. Click **"Upload"**
5. Payment status changes to **"Submitted"**

**Step 4: Wait for Verification**
- Owner reviews your receipt
- Status updates to **"Paid"** when verified
- Or **"Rejected"** if receipt is invalid
  - If rejected, re-upload correct receipt

#### Payment Status Guide

| Status | Description | Action Required |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Pending** | Payment not yet made | Upload payment receipt |
| **Submitted** | Receipt uploaded, pending verification | Wait for owner verification |
| **Paid** | Payment verified and confirmed | No action needed |
| **Rejected** | Receipt invalid or incorrect | Re-upload correct receipt |
| **Overdue** | Past due date, not paid | Make payment immediately |
| **Expired** | Booking canceled, payment void | No action needed |

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Problems

**Issue: "Invalid email or password"**

Solutions:
1. Check caps lock is off
2. Verify email spelling
3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cache/cookies
5. Try different browser

**Issue: "Account not verified"**

Solutions (Owner):
- Wait for admin verification (1-3 business days)
- Check email for verification status
- Contact admin if delayed
- Ensure documents were uploaded correctly

#### Booking Issues

**Issue: "Room not available"**

Reasons:
- Room fully booked for selected dates
- Room under maintenance
- Dorm temporarily closed

Solutions:
- Try different dates
- Select different room type
- Contact owner for availability
- Browse alternative dorms

**Issue: Booking stuck in "Pending"**

Solutions:
- Wait 24-48 hours for owner response
- Send message to owner as reminder
- Check if owner is verified
- Contact admin if no response after 3 days

#### Payment Problems

**Issue: Receipt upload failed**

Solutions:
1. Check file size (max 5MB)
2. Use JPG/PNG format
3. Check internet connection
4. Retry upload
5. Try from different device

**Issue: Payment marked as "Rejected"**

Reasons:
- Wrong amount shown in receipt
- Receipt too old
- Not sent to correct account
- Unclear/unreadable receipt

Solutions:
- Upload correct receipt
- Ensure amount matches exactly
- Use recent transaction
- Take clear photo

#### Mobile App Issues

**Issue: App crashing**

Solutions:
1. Update to latest version
2. Clear app cache
3. Restart device
4. Reinstall app
5. Check storage space

**Issue: "Near Me" not working**

Solutions:
1. Enable location services
2. Grant app location permission
3. Check GPS is enabled
4. Move to open area (better GPS signal)
5. Restart app

**Issue: Push notifications not received**

Solutions:
1. Enable notifications in phone settings
2. Check app notification settings
3. Ensure app is not in battery saver mode
4. Log out and log in again
5. Reinstall app

## ‚ùì FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is LCozy free to use?**  
A: Yes, the platform is free for students and owners. We don't charge service fees for bookings or payments.

**Q: How secure is my information?**  
A: We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/HTTPS) for all data transmission. Personal information is never shared with third parties without consent.

**Q: Can I use LCozy on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes, you can access your account from any device. Your data syncs automatically.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Students pay directly to dorm owners via bank transfer, GCash, or PayMaya. Upload the receipt as proof of payment.

---

### Student FAQs

**Q: How long does booking approval take?**  
A: Typically 24-48 hours. Owners receive instant notifications and usually respond quickly.

**Q: Can I cancel my booking?**  
A: Yes, but only if status is "Pending". Once approved, contact the owner to discuss cancellation.

**Q: What if my payment receipt is rejected?**  
A: Common reasons are wrong amount or unclear image. Upload a correct, clear receipt showing the right transaction.

**Q: Can I book multiple rooms?**  
A: Yes, you can create separate bookings for different rooms or dorms.

**Q: How do I know if a dorm is legitimate?**  
A: Look for the "Verified" badge. This means the owner and property have been verified by admins.

**Q: Can I visit the dorm before booking?**  
A: Yes, use the messaging system to arrange a viewing with the owner.

**Q: What happens if I'm not satisfied with the dorm?**  
A: Contact the owner first to resolve issues. If unresolved, contact admin support. You can also leave a review after checkout.

**Q: Are deposits refundable?**  
A: Depends on the dorm's policy (shown in booking details). Typically refundable if room is left in good condition.

---

### Owner FAQs

**Q: How much does it cost to list my dorm?**  
A: Listing is completely free. No commissions or hidden fees.

**Q: How long does verification take?**  
A: Usually 1-3 business days after submitting complete documents.

**Q: Can I manage multiple dorms?**  
A: Yes, add and manage unlimited dorms from your account.

**Q: How do I receive payments?**  
A: Students pay directly to your bank account/e-wallet. Upload your account details in your profile.

**Q: Can I set my own prices?**  
A: Yes, you have complete control over room pricing.

**Q: What if a student doesn't pay?**  
A: Use the messaging system to send reminders. For persistent issues, contact admin support.

**Q: Can I edit my dorm information after listing?**  
A: Yes, you can edit dorm details, photos, and pricing anytime.

**Q: How do I handle bad reviews?**  
A: You can report inappropriate reviews to admins. Legitimate negative reviews should be addressed by improving your service.

**Q: Can I require students to view the property first?**  
A: Yes, you can communicate this requirement via messages before approving bookings.
